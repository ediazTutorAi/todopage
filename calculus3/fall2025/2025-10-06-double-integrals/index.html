5.1 Double Integration over Rectangular Regions
Definition
The double integral of the function $f(x,y)$ over the rectangular region R in the xy-plane is defined $$\iint_{R} f(x,y,z) \, dV \, dA=\lim_{m,n \to \infty}\sum_{i=1}^m\sum_{j=1}^n f(x_i^*,y_j^*)\delta A $$
